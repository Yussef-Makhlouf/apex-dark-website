Our Story

From Code
to Culture.

APEX started as a one-person freelance operation in 2022, building websites for local Egyptian businesses. Three years later, we're a lean senior team serving clients across the Gulf and MENA region.

We noticed most agencies treated Arabic as an afterthought — broken RTL, non-existent bilingual design. We built our practice around the opposite philosophy: Arabic-first, mobile-first, performance-first.

Today we work as embedded technical partners for marketing agencies, SaaS startups, and growing enterprises who need senior engineering without the overhead of a full team.

"The Gulf market moves fast. Our clients can't afford slow, bloated agencies. They need a partner who decides quickly, builds fast, and stays accountable."
